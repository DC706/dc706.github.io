WELCOME TO DC706


Founded at the start of 2019, we are a community driven group, focused on growing hackers in our local area. We hope to draw attention to skillsets in reverse engineering, malware analysis, and offensive security, but we don't intend to stop there. Some of us dabble in hardware, locksport, cryptography...you name it we want to help each other grow in it!
Join us, hack all the things, and meet some great people while you do it. Check out our first meeting here!
