The next DC706 meeting will be on December 28, 2019 at the Columbus Public Library in the Rothschild Technology Room. DC706 meetings will be a casual affair with no membership dues. All skill levels welcomed and encouraged. The plan for the this meeting is to have a small lockpick village and Network King of the Hill (NetKotH) competition. Locks and lockpicks will be available for the village, but feel free to bring your own. Do bring your own laptop to compete in the NetKotH competition. This meeting will be a simple hacker holiday gathering. No talks, no special events, just all the aforementioned standard activities. Come and hang out!
