Our next DC706 meeting will occur May 25, 2019 at Midtown Coffee House. As usual, all skill and interest levels are welcomed and encouraged. Occurring at the same time as the competition will be a presentation. Presentation will be "VBA Stomping: A Brief Introduction" by Christopher Lamberson
