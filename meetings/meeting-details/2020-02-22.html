The first DC706 meeting will be on February 22, 2020 at Lemongrass Thai & Sushi. DC706 meetings will be a casual affair with no membership dues. All skill levels welcomed and encouraged.The plan for the first meeting is to have a small lockpick village and Network King of the Hill (NetKotH) competition. Locks and lockpicks will be available for the village, but feel free to bring your own. Do bring your own laptop to compete in the NetKotH competition. Current activity is TBA but expect either a fun little CTF event or a talk on SAST, or perhaps even both! Stay tuned for updates :)
